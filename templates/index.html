About Us

Jay is a managing partner at Maze Recruiting Inc. A recruitment firm dedicated to helping working professionals find the right job.

Maze Recruiting is a division of the Bay Talent Group, that specializes in recruiting IT talent for companies in the greater Toronto area (GTA). Bay Talent Group is a premier global human capital company dedicated to the formation, by acquisition and operation of boutique Executive Search, Staffing & Consulting firms that specialize in a full range of professional services; including Accounting, Finance, IT, Legal, HR, and more.
